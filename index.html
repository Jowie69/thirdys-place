Your Home Under
Albay's Night Sky

Nestled in the historic Old Albay district of Legazpi City, Thirdy's Place Hostel wraps every guest in warm Filipino hospitality. Like the crescent moon in our logo, we're here to guide you through one of the Philippines' most breathtaking regions.

From solo backpackers to families — wake up to the majestic cone of Mayon Volcano, explore the iconic Cagsawa Ruins, and return each evening to a cozy, affordable sanctuary under the stars.

FAQ

Common Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times?+
Check-in is at 1:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 NN (noon). Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request. If arriving after 6:00 AM, please inform the property in advance.
Is free parking available?+
Yes! Free private on-site parking is available for all guests, on a first-come, first-served basis.
How far is Mayon Volcano from the hostel?+
Mayon Volcano is approximately 7.7 miles (12 km) away. Day tours can be arranged — our staff are happy to recommend trusted local guides!
Are children welcome?+
Absolutely! Children of all ages are welcome. Please mention the number and ages of children when booking. Extra beds for adults are available at ₱250 per person per night.
What payment methods are accepted?+
On-site payments are accepted in cash (Philippine Peso). Online bookings through Agoda, Booking.com, and other platforms use their own payment systems.
How far is Bicol International Airport?+
Bicol International Airport is approximately 5.6–9 km from the hostel — typically a 15–20 minute drive.
